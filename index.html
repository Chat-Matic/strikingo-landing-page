How can I use the AI assistant for free?

Our General AI Assistant powered by ChatGPT-4o is available for free. You can engage in real-time conversations, access knowledge, and get assistance with various tasks without any cost.

How does the pronunciation assessment work?

Strikingo's pronunciation assessment feature provides real-time feedback to help you improve your speaking skills. It is especially useful for language learners and professionals aiming to enhance their pronunciation.

Yes, with our Personalize Assistant feature, you can create your own chatbot and upload your data for a fully customized AI experience.
